Soviet History through Textbooks

Overview:

This is a project for Digital Methods in Humanistic research

Project Members:

Arina Isayev, Olga Tseytlin, Scott Whalen, Lauren Hinrichsen.

Our Corpus:

We're using the textbook Кратктй Курс Истории СССР (A Short Lesson on the History of the USSR): a textbook printed in 1955 for third and fourth grade Soviet history classes.

Reseach Question:

How to format and annotate textbooks on Soviet History in a way that can be more understandable to a Western audience/ How do various textbooks talk about the same subjects (tentative - we shall see how many we get to)
